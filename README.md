<h2 align="center"><a id="About"></a>
<strong>ğŸ“±About</strong></h2>
<ul>
 	<li>MyFitZ allows you to have a mobile visualization of your personal wardrobe, as well as a reference to a wardrobe wishlist, so you always have quick and easy access to what's in your closet!</li>
 	<li>With MyFitZ, setting up outfits for any occasion will be fast and easy!</li>
 	<li>MyFitZ integrates automated techniques to provide a variety of ways to organize and maintain your wardrobe.</li>
 	<li>Forgetting whether that shirt you want to pair with your cool new jeans is light blue or dark blue is a thing of the past.</li>
 	<li>MyFitZ is a new age method of managing your wardrobe. The app saves you time, energy and provides the comfort of knowing exactly what is in your closet!</li>
 	<li>MyFitZ is perfect for: Kids (of all ages), Clothing/Fashion Designers, Business Professionals, Fashion Enthusiasts, or anyone who wants their wardrobe organized!</li>
</ul>
